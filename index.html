Due to iOS security restrictions, web apps cannot directly access your Music Library.


How to Import:

  1. Open the Music app.
  2. Find the song you want to add.
  3. Tap ... and choose Download.
  4. Export/Share the song to the Files app.
  5. Return here and choose Select from Files.
